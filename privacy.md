# Privacy Policy for Eyeconic TLV WhatsApp Assistant

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Eyeconic TLV ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our automated WhatsApp Assistant (the "Service"). This Service is developed and maintained by Poli Automations on behalf of Eyeconic TLV.

### 1. Information We Collect
When you message our WhatsApp Assistant, we collect:
* **Phone Number:** Your WhatsApp-linked phone number is collected to facilitate communication.
* **Message Content:** We collect the text and media you send to provide customer support, answer product questions, or assist with bookings.
* **Metadata:** Timestamps and delivery status of messages.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
Your data is used strictly for business-specific intents, including:
* Providing information about Eyeconic TLV services (Iris photography, jewelry, and art).
* Assisting with appointment scheduling and pricing inquiries.
* Improving the accuracy of our automated responses.

**Important:** We do not use your personal conversations to train general-purpose AI models. Your data remains siloed to the Eyeconic TLV service environment.

### 3. Data Processing and Third Parties
To provide this Service, we share data with the following categories of service providers:
* **Meta (WhatsApp):** As the platform provider for communication.
* **Poli Automations:** As the technical developer and maintainer of the automation logic.
* **Cloud Infrastructure:** We use **Supabase** for secure data storage and **OpenAI/Anthropic** for processing natural language inquiries.
* **Business Operations:** Data may be shared with Eyeconic TLV staff to complete manual follow-ups or orders.

### 4. Data Retention
We retain chat logs only as long as necessary to fulfill your requests or comply with legal obligations. Standard support logs are typically archived or deleted after 90 days of inactivity.

### 5. Your Rights and Opt-Out
* **Opt-Out:** You can stop all communication at any time by replying "STOP" or blocking the contact in WhatsApp.
* **Data Access/Deletion:** You may request a copy of the data we have stored or ask for its deletion by contacting us at [Insert Eyeconic Email, e.g., Eyeconictlv@gmail.com].

### 6. Compliance with Meta Policies
This assistant is a task-oriented business tool. It is not a general-purpose AI and is restricted to discussing Eyeconic TLV's business operations.
